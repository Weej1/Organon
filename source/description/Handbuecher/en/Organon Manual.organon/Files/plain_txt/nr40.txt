Sort By Time Tag
If chosen, the results will be sorted by time and/or date.
Files, which don't hold a tag for your selection (time, date, time and date), will be left out unless you select 'use untagged files'. These files will be added after the sorted.

Time is sorted from 00:00 to 23:59
Date can be entered from 0.0.0 to 31.12.9999 (DDMMYYYY)
(sorted by YYYYMMDD)
Using date and time the files will be sorted by date and for each date by time.

Entering the date format MMDDYYYY or YYYYMMDD will be added to future versions of Organon.
